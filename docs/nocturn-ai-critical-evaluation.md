You list features (WhatsApp response, email auto-response, web chat) but the REAL benefit is "more direct bookings at higher margins." Your messaging should be 80% outcome, 20% features. Right now it's 50/50.

## ðŸ’€ GRAVEYARD RISKS I SEE

Based on the guide's 10 killers:

### Risk #1: Underpricing (CRITICAL)
Your ROI calculator shows RM 9,315 recovered monthly, but you're probably charging RM 500-800/month. That's only 5-8% of the value created. You should be charging RM 1,500-2,500/month MINIMUM. Hotels that can't afford that aren't your ICP.

### Risk #2: Building horizontal platform instead of vertical solution (MODERATE)
You're trying to be "AI concierge for all hotels." Reality: A city business hotel has different needs than a beach resort. Pick ONE type of hotel and nail it completely first.

### Risk #3: Distribution delusion (CRITICAL)
How are you actually reaching GMs? Your website is good but hotels aren't searching for "AI concierge." They're searching for "increase hotel bookings" or "reduce OTA commissions." Your SEO/content strategy isn't visible.

### Risk #4: Premature feature sprawl
WhatsApp + Email + Web Chat + Lead Dashboard + Human Handoff is A LOT for an MVP. Did you validate that hotels need ALL of these or did you build them because they seemed cool?

### Risk #5: Ignoring the "integration tax"
Hotels use PMS systems (Opera, Protel, SiteMinder). If your AI can't check real-time availability and push confirmed bookings into their PMS, you're just creating more manual work (staff now has to check the AI dashboard AND the PMS).
