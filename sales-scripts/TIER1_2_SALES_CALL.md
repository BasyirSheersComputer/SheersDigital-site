# Tier 1 (Quick Win) - Sales Call Script
## 10-Minute Discovery Call ‚Üí Close or Advance

**Target ICP**: Single/small chains (1-3 outlets), RM 50-200k/mo revenue  
**Decision Maker**: Owner/Founder  
**Call Duration**: 10-15 minutes (max 20 if hot prospect)  
**Goal**: Qualify, Present, Close OR Schedule Proposal Call

---

## üéØ CALL OBJECTIVES

**Primary Goals**:
1. Understand their business and confirm fit
2. Identify dream outcome and pain points
3. Present Quick Win solution
4. Handle objections
5. **Close the deal** or schedule proposal call

**Success Metrics**:
- 80%+ of calls should be qualified opportunities
- 40-60% should close or advance to proposal
- 10-20% should close on first call

---
