# Tier 2 (Growth System) - Sales Call Script
## 15-20 Minute Strategic Discovery Call ‚Üí Multi-Location Solution

**Target ICP**: Multi-location chains (3-10 outlets), RM 200k-1M/mo revenue  
**Decision Makers**: Owner + Ops Manager (often both on call)  
**Call Duration**: 15-25 minutes  
**Goal**: Qualify, Present Growth System, Advance to Proposal/Pilot

---

## üéØ CALL OBJECTIVES

**Primary Goals**:
1. Understand multi-location operational challenges
2. Identify scalability pain points and dream outcomes
3. Present Growth System (all 5 solutions)
4. Handle objections with enterprise-level proof
5. **Advance to pilot program or proposal**

**Success Metrics**:
- 70%+ of calls should be qualified (higher bar than Tier 1)
- 50-70% should advance to proposal/pilot
- 10-20% should commit to pilot on first call

**Key Difference from Tier 1**:
- More strategic (not just tactical waste reduction)
- Pilot program often needed (not immediate close)
- Multiple stakeholders (need buy-in)
- Higher value, longer sales cycle

---

## üìã PRE-CALL PREPARATION (10-15 Minutes)

### Deep Research Required:

- [ ] Confirm exact number of outlets (check all sources)
- [ ] Estimate revenue per outlet and total
- [ ] Identify all decision makers (Owner, Ops Manager, CFO?)
- [ ] Map out locations (which cities, clustered or spread?)
- [ ] Check current POS system (for integration planning)
- [ ] Review any expansion news (opening new outlets?)
- [ ] Understand cuisine/concept (helps with examples)
- [ ] Prepare 2-3 case studies from similar chains
- [ ] Review Growth System details (all 5 solutions)
- [ ] Calculate potential ROI specific to their scale
