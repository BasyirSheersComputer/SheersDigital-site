# Tier 3 (Enterprise) - Executive Briefing Script
## 30-45 Minute Strategic Discussion ‚Üí Pilot/POC Agreement

**Target ICP**: Large chains (10+ outlets), RM 1M+/mo revenue  
**Attendees**: CEO/COO/CFO, possibly Board member  
**Format**: Formal presentation + discussion  
**Goal**: Advance to Pilot/POC or formal RFP process

---

## üéØ EXECUTIVE BRIEFING OBJECTIVES

**Primary Goals**:
1. Understand enterprise strategic priorities
2. Align solution with board-level objectives
3. Present enterprise transformation opportunity
4. Propose pilot/proof-of-concept program
5. **Advance to pilot OR formal procurement process**

**Success Criteria**:
- 60%+ advance to pilot/POC
- 30%+ enter formal procurement
- 10%+ reject (too aggressive selling)

**Key Mindset**: You're presenting to C-level. Be strategic, not tactical.
