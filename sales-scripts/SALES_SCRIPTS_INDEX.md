‚úÖ **Personalize every message** (research, use their name, specific details)  
‚úÖ **Lead with outcomes** (save RM X, reduce Y%)  
‚úÖ **Quantify everything** (specific numbers, not ranges)  
‚úÖ **Remove risk** (guarantees, trial periods)  
‚úÖ **Ask for close** (don't wait for them to volunteer)  
‚úÖ **Follow up persistently** (3-10 touches depending on tier)  
‚úÖ **Track religiously** (CRM, spreadsheet, notes)

‚ùå **Generic messages** (instant delete)

‚ùå **Giving up early** (most sales happen after touch 3-5)
