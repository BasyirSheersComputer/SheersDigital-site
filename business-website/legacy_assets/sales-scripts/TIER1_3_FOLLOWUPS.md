# Tier 1 (Quick Win) - Follow-Up Scripts
## After Sales Call ‚Üí Close the Deal

**Target ICP**: Single/small chains (1-3 outlets)  
**Scenario**: Proposal sent, waiting for decision  
**Goal**: Get to YES or NO (no more "maybe")

---

## üéØ FOLLOW-UP OBJECTIVES

**Primary Goal**: Close the deal  
**Secondary Goal**: Get clear YES or NO (remove from pipeline if NO)  
**Avoid**: Endless "following up" with no decision

**Timeline**: 3-5 follow-ups over 2 weeks, then move on

---

## üìÖ FOLLOW-UP SEQUENCE TIMELINE

### Follow-Up #1: Same Day (Immediately After Call)
**Channel**: WhatsApp + Email  
**Purpose**: Send promised materials  
**Action**: Send, don't wait for response

### Follow-Up #2: 24 Hours After Call
**Channel**: WhatsApp  
**Purpose**: Confirm receipt, answer questions  
**Action**: Quick check-in

### Follow-Up #3: 3 Days After Call
**Channel**: Phone Call  
**Purpose**: Address concerns, push for decision  
**Action**: Scheduled conversation

### Follow-Up #4: 7 Days After Call
**Channel**: WhatsApp + Email  
**Purpose**: Create urgency, final push  
**Action**: Limited-time incentive or deadline

### Follow-Up #5: 14 Days After Call (FINAL)
**Channel**: WhatsApp  
**Purpose**: Get final YES or NO  
**Action**: Take away the sale
