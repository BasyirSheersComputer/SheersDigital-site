## üéì TIPS FOR SUCCESS

### Do's:
‚úÖ **Be brief** (WhatsApp = 3-4 lines max)  
‚úÖ **Be specific** (RM 15-25k, 20-30%, 60 days)  
‚úÖ **Be personal** (research, use their name)

‚úÖ **Follow up** (3 touches minimum)  

### Don'ts:
‚ùå **Generic messages** ("Dear Sir/Madam")  
‚ùå **Feature dumping** ("AI-powered cloud-based...")  
‚ùå **Being pushy** ("Limited time offer!")  
‚ùå **Long paragraphs** (people won't read)  
‚ùå **Multiple CTAs** ("Call or email or visit our website")  
‚ùå **Giving up too early** (need 3-5 touches)  
‚ùå **Talking too much** (ask questions, listen)
