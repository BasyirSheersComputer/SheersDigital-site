# Tier 3 (Enterprise) - Cold Intro Script  
## Goal: Set 30-Minute Executive Briefing

**Target ICP**: Large chains (10+ outlets), RM 1M+/mo revenue  
**Decision Makers**: C-Suite (CEO, COO, CFO) + Board approval often required  
**Channel**: Email (Primary), LinkedIn (Secondary), Executive Assistant coordination  
**Research Required**: 30-60 minutes per prospect

---

## üéØ ENTERPRISE OUTREACH STRATEGY

**Key Differences from Tier 1/2**:
- **Formal process** (not casual WhatsApp)
- **Multiple gatekeepers** (EAs, procurement)
- **Long sales cycle** (2-6 months)
- **Committee decisions** (not individual)
- **RFP process** (often required)
- **Vendor vetting** (security, compliance, stability)

**Success Metrics**:
- **5-15% response rate** (vs 25-35% Tier 1)
- **2-6 month sales cycle** (vs 2-4 weeks Tier 1)
- **Custom pricing** (RM 15k-50k+/month)
- **Higher close value** (RM 180k-600k+ annually)

‚úÖ **Technology Landscape**
- Current ERP system
- POS systems across outlets

‚úÖ **Decision Makers**
- CEO/Founder (visionary, strategic)

## üìä ENTERPRISE RESPONSE PATTERNS

**Immediate Response** (5-10%):

- Happy with current solution
- No budget
- Internal development
- Not interested in vendors
